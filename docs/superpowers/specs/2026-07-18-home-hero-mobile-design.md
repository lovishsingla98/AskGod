# Home Hero and Mobile Responsiveness Design

## Goal

Update the home hero with the approved faith-centered message and make the existing AskGod experience polished and usable on phone-sized screens without changing its visual identity.

## Approved copy

- Heading: “Feeling anxious, stressed, or struggling with financial or relationship problems?”
- Supporting line: “Ask God and let Him answer through His divine books.”
- Belief line: “Solution to all your problems, only if you believe in Him.”

## Responsive behavior

The desktop composition remains unchanged. Below 800px, navigation spacing, page margins, typography, search controls, quick tags, result header, library cards, footer spacing, and the stacked parchment reader scale to the viewport. The reader remains a complete chapter with the selected verse highlighted; its mobile form uses two vertically stacked, independently readable parchment panels instead of the desktop 3D spread.

## Verification

Automated tests and production build must pass. Browser checks cover the home hero and absence of horizontal overflow at representative phone and desktop widths, plus a mobile search-result rendering check.
